DEVICE: Infinix Note 30 (X6833B)

Important: check your current firmware version from build number in settings, then download the respective firmware, then extract the vbmeta.img from it (also boot.img if you need magisk root)

Requirements before flashing

  • Unlocked bootloader of the device
  • PC with ADB and Fastboot installed (it must have USB 2.0 port)
  • Out of box cable or high quality data transferring cable
  • vbmeta.img from your current firmware

The link to download all the latest GSI or CUSTOM ROM:

  • Latest ROMS/Recovery
  • Dobro gsi
  • Github GSI
  • Mystic GSi

Rooting of the Device

  • Unlocked Bootloader

    Make sure your bootloader is unlocked before continuing

  • Copy the boot.img

    Copy the Boot.img from your stock firmware the firmware version should be the same as your build number otherwise you will end up on bootloop

  • Install Magisk app

    Download and install Magisk on your phone from magisk or magisk delta

  • Patch the boot.img

    Go to Magisk and click install choose "Select and Patch a File" in method and select the boot.img the file should be renamed magisk_patched_[Random Strings].img at the download folder of your device.

  • Move the patched boot image to pc

    Copy the magisk_patched_[Random Strings].img to the platform-tools folder in your computer and rename it to "boot.img" you can copy the vbmeta.img from your firmware if you want to disable vbmeta

  • Add patched img to platform-tools

    On your computer open the platform-tool folder and right click on an empty space then open in terminal

  • Check adb drivers

    Check if adb is working and device is shown to the pc
    adb devices

  • Reboot your phone in fastboot mode

    adb reboot bootloader

  • Check fastboot drivers

    fastboot devices

  • Flash patched_boot.img

    If you flashed wrong boot.img to boot partition you will get bricked
    fastboot flash boot patched_boot.img

  • Reboot to system

    fastboot reboot

  • Open magisk app

    Simply it will say to restart device for the root toi be complete and you are all SET!!
